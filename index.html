Phabalicious: Supports your deployments and every-day devops-tasks

Get started

  • All your instances

    Store all necessary configuration for all your instances inside your project-folder.

  • Interact with your app

    Reset, backup or deploy your application, copy data between instances or run custom scripts.

  • Scaffold to the rescue

    Scaffold configuration-files, new projects, create or destroy new instances of your app.

  • Administer your app via its hosting-environment

    Administer and deploy your application-stack via kubernetes, docker or docker-composer.

  • Artifact-based deployments

    Build your app with all dependencies and push the artifacts down the pipeline.

  • A local dev-environment

    An optional, docker-based local dev-environment. Open for all (docker-based) stacks.
